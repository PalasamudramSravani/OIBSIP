Sundar Pichai

Biography

Sundar Pichai, born as Pichai Sundararajan in Chennai, India, is one of the most influential figures in the global technology industry. Growing up in a modest environment, he developed a strong interest in academics and problem-solving from a young age.

He joined Google in 2004 and played a key role in developing Google Chrome, which later became the most widely used web browser in the world. His ability to create simple and effective products helped him rise quickly within the company.

Over time, he led major products like Gmail, Google Maps, and Android. In 2015, he became the CEO of Google, and in 2019, he became the CEO of Alphabet Inc., leading innovation in artificial intelligence and global technology.

Sundar Pichai is known for his calm leadership style and vision. His journey from a simple background to leading one of the world’s biggest tech companies inspires millions of people around the world.
